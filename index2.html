I came from a small town in South Jersey. I was born and raised there and all of my best memories are from that little town. The smallness of the community is what I believe made me the person I am today. It gave me close friendships that (I hope) will last a life time and also made my family grow closer. As a person I describe myself as fun, free flowing, wild, and caring. Living in the woods of south jersey made me cherish the small moments. My friends and my family are the most important things in my life. Without them I would be lost. The following two videos are homemade videos my friend made from our home football games last year. Friday night lights at our school was bigger than the football team. Every friday the whole school and community came together to cheer, laugh, and have the time of our lives. These videos show some of the happiest moments during my time in Shamong.
